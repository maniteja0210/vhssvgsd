Our Story

Born from a single idea: coffee can connect. Cozy Brew started as a weekend pop-up where friends and neighbors gathered to share stories. Today, that spirit lives in every roast, every latte, and every seat we set aside for someone to feel at home.

  • •
    Third-wave craft: We source direct from farmers focused on quality and traceability.
  • •
    Community-first: Monthly events, barista training, and local collaborations.
  • •
    Sustainability: Eco packaging and responsible practices.

Our Roasting Philosophy

We roast to highlight origin, not to mask it. Lighter profiles for floral and citrus notes. Medium for balanced complexity. Dark only where it serves the cup. Each roast is cupped and adjusted until it sings.
